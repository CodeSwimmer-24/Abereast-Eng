About us

Founded in 1974, Abreast Engineering Company is a Jamshedpur-based firm offering comprehensive mechanical and engineering services. With over 1000 skilled professionals and a turnover of ₹30 Crores, the company operates across major industrial sites including Tata Steel, Hindustan Copper Limited, IOCL, and more.

Their mission is to be a single-window solution for long-term client partnerships, delivering quality products and services with cost-effectiveness and reliability.
